Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

SourcifyDigital ("we", "our", or "us") operates the SourcifyDigital platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our services:

  • Account information: Name, email address, and business details you provide when creating an account.
  • Financial data: With your explicit consent, we may connect to your financial institutions via Stripe Financial Connections to access account balances, transaction history, and account ownership details. This data is used solely for providing you with financial insights and reports.
  • Usage data: Basic analytics about how you interact with our platform to improve user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our financial management services
  • Generate financial reports and insights you request
  • Communicate with you about your account and service updates
  • Detect and prevent unauthorized access or fraudulent activity

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal or financial data. We only share data with:

  • Stripe Inc. — Our financial data processor. Financial data is stored and processed via Stripe's Financial Connections service under their security and compliance framework. Your bank credentials are never visible to us.
  • Service providers — Only as necessary for hosting, analytics, and infrastructure (e.g., Vercel, Google Cloud). These providers are bound by data processing agreements.
  • Legal requirements — If required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • All data transmitted via TLS/SSL encryption
  • API keys stored as restricted keys with limited permissions
  • No storage of bank login credentials — authentication is handled entirely by Stripe
  • Regular security reviews of our infrastructure

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. Financial connection data is subject to Stripe's retention policies.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and export your data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Revoke financial data access at any time via Stripe's dashboard
